# Society-Level Synthesis (Sample)

## Macro Snapshot
- Attention markets (memes/CT) move fastest, but are least durable.
- Institutional segments move slowest, but define long-term adoption.
- Silent majority drives retention; they don’t post but decide outcomes.

## What This Means for Product
- Build for **trust + reliability** while tracking **attention + narrative**.
- Convert loud users into signals, not requirements.
- Use behavioral data to validate or reject CT-driven assumptions.
