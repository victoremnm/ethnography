# Ethics

This repo follows a netnography-informed ethics stance:
- No doxxing, no private info, no harassment.
- Use public sources only; anonymize when appropriate.
- Avoid targeting individuals; focus on patterns and systems.
- Note analyst bias; separate observation from speculation.
- Prefer consent if direct engagement is required.

## Guiding principles (updated charter)
- **Selectivity matters.** Say yes to fewer projects/communities to choose congruence over noise.
- **Solitude helps.** Time alone clarifies signal from noise and protects deeper thinking.
- **Agency is a bias.** Default to action/accountability, but recognize structural limits.
- **Clarity before commitment (when possible).** Seek clarity, but don’t let it become paralysis.
- **Construction can drift into optimization.** Build identity without turning life into a perpetual pipeline.
- **Self‑worth needs an anchor.** Achievement is fragile; hold a deeper base than outcomes.
- **Solitude can become armor.** Use it to think and rest, not to disappear.
- **Selectivity can become rigidity.** Keep standards without avoiding growth through discomfort.
- **Responsibility has limits.** Hold accountability and humility together.
- **Some decisions clarify only after action.** Learn when to move through fog.
- **Carry forward:** construct without over‑optimizing, choose standards without becoming a cage, accept randomness, and build a life that belongs to something beyond you.
