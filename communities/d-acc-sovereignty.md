# d/acc & Computing Self-Sovereignty (2026)

## The Thesis: "Reclaiming the Cypherpunk Soul"

In 2026, the **d/acc (Defensive/Decentralized Accelerationism)** movement has moved from theory to protocol. Championed by Vitalik Buterin, it argues that we must accelerate technologies that favor **defense, privacy, and sovereignty** to buffer against both Big Tech and "Agentic Malware."

- **The Core Metric:** **The "Walkaway Test"** — Can the community survive if the founders/centralized infra providers exit?
- **The Goal:** A "Switzerland-like" digital world (defensible and neutral).

## Key 2026 Nodes & Milestones

### 1. The Hegotá Hard Fork (Late 2026)
The landmark upgrade for Ethereum's "Epistemic Hardening."
- **FOCIL (Fork-Choice Enforced Inclusion Lists):** Protocol-level censorship resistance. Validators can no longer arbitrarily block transactions, making the "World Computer" truly neutral.
- **ZK-Everything:** Transitioning to ZK-EVM verification as the default, allowing for "Local Verification" on consumer hardware.

### 2. The Farcaster "Great Return" (Jan 2026)
The Merkle Manufactory team returned $180M in VC funding and shifted Warpcast to Neynar.
- **The Signal:** End of the "VC Social Unicorn" era. Farcaster is now viewed as a durable **identity and developer layer** rather than a "Twitter-killer."
- **Cultural Shift:** From "SocialFi" (speculation) to **"Epistemic Infrastructure"** (verifiable social data).

### 3. Privacy-as-a-Service (PaaS)
The rise of **Gated Shielded Pools** and **Stealth Addresses**.
- **The Tension:** Balancing the 2026 "Agentic Economy" (machine transactions) with the need for **Institutional Privacy** (auditability without exposure).

## Contested Views: Building Around the Periphery

*Deferring to the primary agent's findings on the "Agentic Web," we must contest the "Defense" assumption:*

### The "Defense-Offense" Fallacy
A core adversarial critique of d/acc: **Every defensive shield is a component of a future offensive weapon.**
- **Fact-Check:** The same ZK-proofs that protect user privacy in 2026 are being used to build "Privacy-Gated Malware" that is undetectable by traditional security audits.
- **Refute:** d/acc is not "safe acceleration"; it is the **democratization of advanced weapons.** By making "Computing Sovereignty" a default, we are giving malicious AI agents the same "unwavering resistance to censorship" we intended for humans.

### The "Sovereignty Illusion"
- **The Cost of Sovereignty:** Running a "Hardened" 2026 node is still expensive ($500k+/year for a full Farcaster Hub).
- **Conclusion:** Self-sovereignty is becoming a **"Luxury Belief"** for the digital elite. The "Masses" remain tethered to centralized convenience (Google/OpenAI) because the "Hardened" path is too difficult/costly for the average user.
