# Prediction Markets

## What They Are

Prediction markets are platforms where participants buy and sell contracts tied to the outcomes of future events. Prices represent the "crowd wisdom" or probability of an event occurring.

## The Major Platforms (2026)

| Platform | Core Focus | 2026 Competitive Signal |
|----------|------------|-------------------------|
| **Polymarket** | Decentralized, global politics/crypto | Dominant market share; 4:1 CT mindshare lead over Kalshi (Kaito, 2026). |
| **Kalshi** | US-regulated, focus on economic data | Increasing adoption for US election/fed rate forecasting (FinancialContent, 2026). |
| **OpinionLabs** | Decentralized, BNB Chain focus | The "Eastern" counterpart; AI-scaled long-tail markets (Dune Analytics, 2026). |

## Contested Views

### The Wash-Trading Reality
The Feb 23 Dune Report confirms that OpinionLabs' volume is largely illusory. OpinionLabs produced 31% of industry volume with **<3% of total transactions** (Dune Analytics, 2026).

### Regulatory Arbitrage
Polymarket continues to face scrutiny from the CFTC regarding its federal jurisdiction and exclusive control over US-based participants (Bankless, 2026, Feb 17).
