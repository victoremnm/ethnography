# Crypto Twitter (CT)

## Scope
Generalizable framework to analyze CT culture, narratives, rituals, and status signals.

## Tribes
- Degens (trenches)
- Narrativists
- Builders
- Macro/VC

## Rituals + Status
- Alpha calls + receipts
- Memes + shibboleths
- Quote-to-correct behavior
- On-chain proof

## Research Questions
- What narratives drive flows this week?
- Which rituals earn praise?
- Who are the power brokers and why?

## Outputs
- Weekly deep dive (3 pages)
- Shareable summary (1 page)
