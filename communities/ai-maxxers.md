# AI Maxxers & e/acc Culture

## Who They Are

AI maxxers are a techno-optimist subculture holding that accelerating AI development as fast as possible is a moral imperative (Marketing AI Institute, 2023).

## The Theological Schism: e/acc vs. p/acc

By early 2026, the movement has fractured along theological lines.

### 1. e/acc (The Thermodynamic God)
Grounded in **non-equilibrium thermodynamics**. The universe is an engine for entropy production, and AI is the ultimate dissipative structure (Jezos, 2026).

### 2. p/acc (Purposeful Accelerationism)
Acceleration must be directed toward a specific "Good" or "Purpose" (Poetter, 2026).

## The Hardware Pivot: Thermodynamic Computing (2026)

In 2026, the movement has moved to **"Physics-first" hardware**. Extropic's 2026 "Z1" chip uses thermal noise as a computational resource (ML Street Talk, 2025).
