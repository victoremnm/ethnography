# Institutional / Risk-Averse

## Scope
Organizations with compliance, governance, and reputational constraints.

## Traits
- Longer decision cycles
- Emphasis on auditability, stability, SLAs
- Avoids hype; requires evidence and accountability

## Research Questions
- What proof lowers perceived risk?
- Which assurances (docs, audits, SLAs) matter most?
- What is the procurement path?
